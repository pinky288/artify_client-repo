 Artify



Artify is a modern art showcase platform where artists can share their artworks and users can explore, like, and favorite artworks.

 Features:

Artworks: Browse a curated collection of artworks from various artists.

Top Artists & Community Highlights: Featured section showcasing top artists and community contributions.
Interactive Art Cards: View details, like, and add artworks to your favorites.

Responsive Design: Fully responsive layout for desktop and mobile devices.

**User Engagement: Users can interact with artworks, favorite them, and keep track of their liked items.
